Predicting customer adherence to brand product

Interpreting Drivers

After discovering the customers showing low adherence, I did a driver analysis to identify and analyze key drivers by utilizing secondary datasets such as patient demographics, purchase patterns etc. which could provide valuable insights that cause low adherence among customers of Product C. I also looked at customer segmentation based on key behaviors and cohort analysis for improved customer adherence interpretation.

I followed a 4 step approach for this exercise -

1. Hypothesis selection and feature engineering

2. Descriptive and inferential analysis

3. Machine learning based drivers' analysis

4. Customer archetypes and cohort analysis

Through this work, I was able to uncover the sources of business for Product C and create persistency curves to understand drop off rate in early months and identify the customer archetypes to be tagged as non-persistent.
